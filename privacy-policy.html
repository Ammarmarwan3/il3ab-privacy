Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026

1. Introduction

il3ab ("we," "our," or "us") operates a mobile application for booking sports courts in the Bekaa region of Lebanon. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using il3ab, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information we collect

We collect the following types of information:

Account information: When you create an account, we collect your phone number, full name, and optional profile photo. Venue owners additionally provide their business name and venue details.

Location data: With your permission, we collect your device's location to show you nearby sports courts. You can disable location access at any time through your device settings.

Booking data: We store information about your court bookings, including dates, times, courts booked, payment method selected, and booking status.

Communications: If you use the in-app match chat feature, we store messages exchanged between players within a booking group.

Device information: We collect device identifiers and push notification tokens to send you booking confirmations and updates.

Photos: If you upload a profile photo or venue photos, these are stored securely in our cloud storage.

3. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the il3ab app
  • Process and manage your court bookings
  • Show you nearby sports courts based on your location
  • Send push notifications about booking confirmations, cancellations, match updates, and tournament updates
  • Enable communication between players through the match chat feature
  • Connect players with venue owners for booking-related inquiries
  • Provide customer support

4. Information sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited information in the following cases:

  • With venue owners: When you make a booking, the venue owner can see your name, phone number, and booking details to facilitate your visit.
  • With other players: If you join an open match, other players in that match can see your name and communicate with you through the in-app chat.
  • Service providers: We use Supabase for our backend infrastructure and Expo for push notifications. These providers process data on our behalf and are bound by their own privacy policies.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or in response to valid legal requests.

5. Payment information

il3ab does not directly process or store credit card numbers or financial account information. When paying via cash, no financial data is collected. When using Whish Money (when available), payment is processed entirely through the Whish Money platform, and their privacy policy governs that transaction.

6. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely on servers hosted by Supabase in Frankfurt, Germany (EU). We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption in transit (TLS/SSL) and row-level security policies on our database.

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data: View your profile and booking history within the app.
  • Update your data: Edit your profile information at any time.
  • Delete your account: You can delete your account from the Profile settings. This will permanently remove your profile data. Note that some booking records may be retained for venue owner records.
  • Disable location: You can revoke location permissions through your device settings at any time.
  • Disable notifications: You can turn off push notifications through your device settings.

8. Children's privacy

il3ab is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the app and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
